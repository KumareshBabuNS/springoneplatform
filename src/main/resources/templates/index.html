Join us for Platform SpringOne

Platform SpringOne brings together the people, process and tools for delivering and operating software services. Learn and share with the startups and enterprises leveraging modern Java with Spring connecting all the pieces of the modern software puzzle from developer, operator, architect, data scientist to executive. If you are looking for training, technical deep dives or cases studies, Platform SpringOne will have something for you. From the cloud native to those just starting to transform, there will be in-depth advanced technical topics and accessible introductory content covering agile, continuous delivery, devops, microservices, data analytics, machine learning, and more.
